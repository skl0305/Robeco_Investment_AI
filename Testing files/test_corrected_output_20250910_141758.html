Additionally, the impact of global supply chain disruptions and inflationary pressures on Apple's cost structure needs to be carefully evaluated. A comprehensive understanding of these factors is essential for determining the fair value of Apple's stock and its suitability for inclusion in our investment portfolio.

LONG TERM OUTLOOK

Apple's long-term outlook hinges on its ability to successfully diversify its revenue streams and maintain its technological edge. The company's investments in augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) technologies hold significant promise, but the timeline for widespread adoption and the potential for competition from other tech giants remain uncertain.
