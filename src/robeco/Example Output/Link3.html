Link Real Estate Investment Trust (Link REIT) is Asia's largest REIT by asset value, managing a diversified portfolio of retail facilities, car parks, offices, and logistics assets across Hong Kong, Mainland China, Australia, Singapore, and the UK, with a total valuation of approximately HK$226 billion as of March 31, 2025. The company operates under an internally managed structure, a key differentiator in the APAC REIT landscape, which mitigates potential conflicts of interest and optimizes cost efficiency. Link REIT's strategic focus on community and neighborhood retail assets in Hong Kong, representing over 50% of its portfolio, provides a resilient income base from non-discretionary spending. We expect continued portfolio diversification and active asset management to drive sustainable revenue growth, despite prevailing macroeconomic headwinds impacting rental reversions in some core markets.
